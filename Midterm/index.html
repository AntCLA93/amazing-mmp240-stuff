The Boys:

2019, TV-18, 4 seasons

"The Boys" is a dark, satirical superhero TV series based on the comic book of the same name by Garth Ennis and Darick Robertson. It explores the idea of superheroes existing in the real world, but with a twist: these heroes, known as "Supes," are not the benevolent protectors people believe them to be. The series is set in a world where superheroes are managed by a powerful corporation called Vought International, which prioritizes profit and public image over true heroism.

Creator: Eric Kripke

Cast: Karl Urban, Jack Quaid, Antony Starr, Erin Moriarty, Dominique McElligott, Jessie T. Usher, Laz Alonso, Tomer Capone, Karen Fukuhara, Chace Crawford, Nathan Mitchell, Colby Minifie

Genre: dark comedy, superhero, action, and satirical drama
